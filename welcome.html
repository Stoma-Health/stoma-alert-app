What it is

A calm and structured place to manage life with a stoma

Between appointments, you manage a lot on your own: skin, comfort, supplies and the odd worry. Stoma Alert gives you a calm, structured place to keep track of it all, keep a clear record for yourself, and get your supplies sorted. It helps you notice and keep track. It doesn't diagnose.

Our promise“Records symptoms and images you can share with a healthcare professional.”

That's the whole philosophy. Stoma Alert helps you keep a clear picture, and your stoma nurse stays the one who reviews and decides. The app never claims to diagnose or replace them. In-app sharing and messaging with your nurse is coming next.

Inside the app

Everything you need nothing you don't

Six things the app does, built around the day-to-day reality of stoma life.

Daily check-in

A few taps each day: how you feel, how your stoma looks, how your abdomen is. Under a minute, and it quietly builds a picture day by day.

Photo diary

An on-screen guide helps you take a clear, consistent photo, so you build a reliable record. Sharing it straight to your stoma nurse is coming next.

Progress over time

See how skin, comfort and mood are trending. Patterns are easy to spot and share, not lost to memory.

Supplies & reorder

Your pouches, baseplates and accessories on file with sizes and cut, reorder in a tap and have them delivered before you run low.

Learn library

Short, practical guides co-produced with stoma nurses: skin care, travelling with confidence, and the things that quietly help.

Community

Peer support from others who understand what stoma life is like. Moderated and safe, launching alongside the app.

Your journey

One companion from pre-op to everyday life

Living with a stoma changes through its stages, especially in the early weeks. Stoma Alert follows you through each one, so it becomes a daily habit rather than a one-off download.

Before surgery

Prepare & reassure

What to expect, gentle pre-op education, and set up your profile so you're ready from day one.

Hospital & discharge

Get set up

Capture your stoma details and first photos, a discharge checklist, and your first product setup.

First 6–8 weeks

Adjust & refit

The stoma shrinks as it heals. Guided refitting and reminders help you keep the right size and a comfortable fit.

Everyday life

Stay on top of it

Daily check-ins, easy reordering, learn guides and community, the reassurance of a routine that keeps working in the background.

Supplies, sorted

Never run low reorder in one tap

In the UK most stoma appliances are free on NHS prescription and delivered to your door. Stoma Alert's job is simply to make the repeat effortless and send it to the supplier you already use.

1

Saved products

Your pouches, baseplates and accessories held on file, with sizes and cut, ready to go.

2

One-tap reorder

Confirm what you need; the app sends the repeat-prescription request on your behalf.

3

Prescription & dispense

Your GP and chosen supplier handle the prescription and dispense it, just as they do today.

4

Delivered to your door

Your supplies arrive at home, discreetly, and before you were going to run out.

Stoma Alert is independent of the bag manufacturers. You keep your own products and your own supplier. The app just takes the hassle out of the repeat.

Coming next
