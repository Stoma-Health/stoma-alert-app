Colour reference card
Print the reference chart

Print it at actual size (100%), cut out the centre circle, then lay the chart flat around your stoma so it shows through the window. Photographing your stoma through the chart lets the app keep colours steady and measure size in millimetres, whatever the lighting.

Plain paper is fine. The printout has a 100 mm line — check it with a ruler so sizes stay accurate. Then tick “I'm using the reference chart” on the camera screen.
